Instructions

Description

This quiz will help you find out if your mathematics & logic skills need practice or refinement. It is worth 2% of your course grade, and you will be given the best mark you achieve out of two attempts. In each attempt you will be given a random selection of ten questions from each of three pools, for a total of 30 questions. You will have 60 minutes to answer the questions, then the quiz will automatically save your answers and submit. You can use any legitimate study materials you want, but you may not use 'cheating' sites like StudyBuddy or CourseHero and you may not collaborate on or discuss quiz questions and answers with other students in any way until you and they have all finished writing the quz and gotten your results. The quiz is available from start of class in the week in which the lesson is made available to you until end of day Sunday after the following week (North American 'Eastern Time' - see note in course syllabus). No late submissions will be accepted without special arrangement.

You should make sure you're in a quiet place with a strong, stable internet connection and your laptop charged or plugged in and fully updated. You can continue the quiz if you lose your Blackboard session in any way, but the countdown timer will continue to run while you re-establish your connection.

You should be sure to study the course material and practice the methods you have learned before you try the quiz.

If you get four or more questions wrong in any one section, or if you get nine or more questions wrong overall, you should seek tutoring in the appropriate area(s) of math & logic skills. Free tutoring is available from the College here.

Your professors will not review the entire quiz in class or share the overall answers, but they will be happy to answer individual queries about specific questions from the test.

Good luck!
